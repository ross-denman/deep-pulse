# 📖 Structural Lexicon (Phase 3)
## The Sovereign Notary's Framework

This document defines the official terminology of the Soul-Ledger repository. We have moved away from "Crypto/Financial" and "Military" metaphors toward a **Forensic-Agricultural** framework. This shifts the focus from "profit and combat" to "truth harvesting and record settling."

### Core Terminology

| Term | Role / Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **The Nexus** | The Master Outpost (OCI Instance). The physical "Home" of the Sovereign Notary. |
| **Sovereign Notary** | The logical Authority/Persona that signs the Chronicle from the Nexus. |
| **Probes** | Remote/Secondary outposts that gather data but do not hold the Master Keys. |
| **The Chronicle** | The immutable JSON-LD ledger. The historical archive of all settled Grains. |
| **Grains of Truth** ($REP-G) | The primary unit of settled intelligence. High-gravity discoveries earned through verifiable auditing. |
| **Settling** | The act of notarizing and finalizing a discovery into the archive. |
| **The Granary** | The physical vault where The Chronicle is stored for long-term safety. |
| **The Discovery Mesh** | The global, interconnected web of Probes and the Nexus. |
