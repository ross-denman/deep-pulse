# AAOS 2026: Ozempic Bone Integrity Audit (OFFICIAL)
**Location**: New Orleans, LA
**Drafting Agency**: Michigan State Orthopedic Research

## Key Findings:
- Study by **Muaaz Wajahath (Michigan State)** confirms high-resolution bone density shifts.
- The viral narrative claiming Ozempic is "**shredding bones**" is inconsistent with clinical data.
- The 5-year study of 73,483 patients shows an absolute increase in osteoporosis from 3.2% to **4.1%** (a **0.9%** absolute delta).
- While the relative risk for **osteomalacia** rose by **2.55x**, it only affects 0.2% of users.
- Total estimated fiscal impact on future reconstructive surgery: **135 billion pounds** (Sterling).

**Verdict**: Signal detected (0.9% increase). Narrative 'shredding' is a high-variance exaggeration (100x magnification).
